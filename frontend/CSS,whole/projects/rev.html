Russian President

Vladimir Putin

Vladimir Putin is the longtime leader of Russia, serving as either president or prime minister since 1999. A former KGB intelligence officer, he rose to power promising stability after the collapse of the Soviet Union. with tight control over the media, opposition. Internationally, he is known for assertive actions, including the annexation of Crimea in 2014 and the invasion of Ukraine in 2022, which led to widespread global condemnation and sanctions.
